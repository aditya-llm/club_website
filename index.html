About The Club

Next Gen Engineers is a student-run club at the Central University of Rajasthan dedicated to fostering a culture of innovation, collaboration, and hands-on learning. We aim to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application by working on real-world projects, hosting workshops, and competing in national-level events. Our mission is to empower the next generation of engineers with the skills and confidence to solve tomorrow's challenges.

A different approach, using a new method of manufacturing.

We are dedicated to pioneering new techniques and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in engineering. Our club focuses on practical, hands-on projects that not only build skills but also solve real-world problems. Discover how we are shaping the future.
